About PWI

Crafting Quality Timber Since 1987

PWI Pvt Ltd is a Sri Lanka-based wood processing and engineering company with over three decades of industry experience. Founded in 1987, we operate at industrial scale — processing Rubber, Mahogany and Pine timber through every stage of production, from raw log sawing to precision-engineered finished products. Our integrated facility combines traditional craftsmanship with modern engineering capability, including CNC machining, pressure treatment, and kiln drying technology.

From supplying the local construction and furniture trade to exporting wooden brush handles to Japan, the United Kingdom, France and Germany, PWI has built a reputation for consistent quality and reliable delivery. Whether you need custom finger joint boards, treated structural timber, or high-volume export-grade handles, our experienced team is equipped to meet your specification — at scale, and on time.

Products & Services

Integrated Wood Processing Capability

PWI offers eight integrated product lines — from raw log conversion through to export-grade finished goods. Each process is carried out within our Sri Lanka facility to consistent quality standards.

01

Wooden Log Sawing

Precision cutting from the very first cut.
Using industrial-grade Wood-Mizer band saws, circular blades and frame saws, PWI converts raw Rubber, Mahogany and Pine logs into accurately dimensioned sawn timber at large-scale production volumes. Our sawing operation is the foundation of every product we manufacture, ensuring consistent grain quality and dimensional accuracy from the outset.

02

Chemical Treatment

Long-lasting protection, engineered into every piece.
Our borax-based pressure impregnation process delivers deep, uniform chemical penetration across sawn timber, providing effective resistance to fungal decay, insect attack and moisture ingress. This treatment is applied under controlled pressure conditions, meeting the durability requirements of both local construction projects and international export specifications.

03

Kiln Drying

Stability starts with the right moisture content.
PWI operates dedicated drying kilns that bring timber to precise, specification-grade moisture levels before further processing or dispatch. Controlled kiln drying eliminates warping, shrinkage and structural instability — ensuring that every board leaving our facility performs reliably in its intended application.

04

Plywood Door Manufacturing

Engineered doors built for performance and longevity.
Our plywood doors are manufactured to exacting structural standards, combining quality timber cores with durable surface veneers. Available in standard and custom dimensions, they are suited to residential, commercial and industrial applications where consistent quality and reliable supply are essential.

05

Wooden Planks

Premium-grade timber for ceilings, floors and wall linings.
PWI produces smooth-finished timber planks from select Rubber, Mahogany and Pine, ready for direct use in ceiling lining, flooring and interior panelling. Each plank is processed through our integrated production chain — sawn, treated, dried and finished — to deliver a product that meets the expectations of professional contractors and developers alike.

06

Finger Joint Boards

Custom-sized engineered boards for demanding applications.
Our finger joint boards are produced by precision-jointing shorter timber sections into full-length, dimensionally stable panels. Available in custom widths, thicknesses and lengths, they are widely used in furniture manufacturing, interior joinery and structural applications where strength, consistency and material efficiency are priorities.

07

CNC Design Boards

Intricate patterns, machined to exact specification.
Utilising computer-controlled routing and carving technology, PWI produces decorative and architectural timber panels to precise client designs. From ornamental motifs to functional perforated panels, our CNC capability allows us to deliver repeatable, high-detail results across both small custom orders and large production runs.

08

Wood Brush Handles

Export-grade handles trusted by markets across four countries.
PWI manufactures wooden brush handles to international quality standards, with an established export record supplying buyers in Japan, the United Kingdom, France and Germany. Produced from select timber species and finished to tight dimensional tolerances, our brush handles represent the precision manufacturing and reliable supply capability that has defined PWI's reputation since 1987.
